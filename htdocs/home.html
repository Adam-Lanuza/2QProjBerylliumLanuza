WELCOME TO THE GAMER'S GUIDEBOOK!

If you are bored and want to try out a new video game, then this site is perfect for you. The main goal of this site is to recommend games that you can try. It has a list of the video games and their respective information like price or platforms. It also includes gaming genres for those who want to seek out games that are like their favorites or for those who want to find out what genre is for them. There is also a compilation of the companies that make our favorite games if you ever want to try their other products. Finally, if ever you are curious about how video games started, then we also have a page dedicated to the history of video games. To find those pages, please follow the link boxes below or in the header bar.
