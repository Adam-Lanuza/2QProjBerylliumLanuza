Sucker Punch Productions

Founded in 1997, Sucker Punch Productions is an American company most known for their hit game, Ghost of Tsushima. It has been a part of Sony Computer Entertainment since 2011. The games they publish are usually either platformers or action-adventure games.

Known Games and Installments
  • Ghost of Tsushima
  • Infamous Series
  • The Sly Collection
  • All Company Games

Mojang Studios

Mojang Studios was created in 2009 by Markus Perrson. It is currently a part of Microsoft and has been a part of it since 2014. The studio is known for inventing Minecraft, an incredibly popular sandbox game but they have also made a few spin-offs like Minecraft: Story Mode and Minecraft: Dungeons.

Known Games and Installments
  • Minecraft
  • Minecraft: Dungeons
  • Minecraft: Education Edition
  • All Company Games

InnerSloth LCC

InnserSloth is known for their two hit games, The Henry Stickin Collection and Among Us. It was formed in 2015, but the company only became popular during the pandemic when many people used Among Us to interact and play with their friends. Since 2020, the company has earned many awards, and has created a large fanbase.

Known Games and Installments
  • Among Us
  • The Henry Stickmin Collection
  • Dig2China
  • All Company Games

Unknown Worlds Entertianment

Unknown Worlds Entertainment is an American company that was founded on 2001. The company started with Natural Selection, a first-person shooter. It soon developed into a full company and created its hit game series Subnautica. The company was recently bought by Krafton on the October of 2021.

Known Games and Installments
  • Subnautica
  • Natural Selection
  • Zen of Sudoku
  • All Company Games

miHoYo

miHoYo is a Chinese company known for anime style games like Genshin Impact and Honkai Impact 3rd. It was formed in 2012 with Zombiegal Kawaii being its first official release. However, the company did create FlyMe2theMoon before being regarded as an official company. Since then, they have remained as an well known company.

Known Games and Installments
  • Genshin Impact
  • Honkai Impact 3rd
  • Tears of Themis
  • All Company Games

Joey Drew Studios

Joey Drew Studios is a subgroup of another company named Kindly Beast and is dedicated to developing the Bendy and the Ink Machine series. The company actually shares its name with the fictional animation studio in the game's story. It was formed on July 2018 and has created many side games that act as extra lore to the main Bendy game.

Known Games and Installments
  • Bendy and the Ink Machine
  • Bendy in Nightmare Run
  • Boris and the Dark Survival
  • All Company Games

Rayark Inc.

Rayark Inc. is a Taiwanese country that was founded on September 2011. The company specializes in creating games for mobile platforms. Usually those games are Rhythm Games but they also have a few role-playing and strategy games. Some of their popular games are the Deemo and Cytus Serieses and Sdorica.

Known Games and Installments
  • Cytus
  • Deemo
  • Sdorica
  • All Company Games

Thekla, Inc.

While not having that many games under its name, Thekla Inc has created some incredible games. They have created two games, the Witness and Braid which are both puzzle solving games. Both of the games were critically acclaimed. The credit to most of the games goes to Jonathan Blow who funded most of the games with his own money.

Known Games and Installments
  • The Witness
  • Braid
