All Games

Ghost of Tsushima
Price 3000 Pesos Rating 9.5/10
Company Sucker Punch Genres Action-adventure, stealth
Internet Requirement For Multiplayer Only Platforms PS4/PS5

In the 13th century, the Mongols plan to invade Japan. Their first stop, the island of Tsushima. To protect his home and family, Jin Sakai embarks on a mission to stop the Mongol invasion. But if he wants to achieve victory, he is going to need every trick he can find. Whether honorable or not. The player controls Jin through his journey meeting many allies and enemies as you explore the beutiful islands.

Minecraft
Price 1000 - 2000 pesos Rating 9/10
Company Mojang Genres Sandbox, survival
Internet Requirement For Mulitiplayer Only Platforms Windows, macOS, Android, iOS, XBox, PlayStation, Nintendo

Minecraft is a game of many possibilities. Do you want to build massive structures like castles or spaceships? Do you want to roleplay with your friends? Do you want some action and the thrill of winning a competition? Well, let's just say that there is a reason that minecraft attracts a wide variety of audiences. Minecraft is a game of creativity. As long as you are creative enough, you can make anything

Among Us
Price Free - 250 pesos Rating 8/10
Company Innersloth Genres Party, Social Deduction
Internet Requirement Yes Platforms Android, iOS, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation, Xbox

Welcome to Among Us! A game where everyone is your enemy. In this game, players are randomly assigned a role as either a crewmate or an imposter. Imposters win by killing all of the crewmates without being caught while crewmates win by discovering and voting out the imposters. You better not play too much though or else you might start seeing something sus all around you.

Subnautica: Below Zero
Price 1500 Pesos Rating 9/10
Company Unknown Worlds Entertainment Genres Action-adventure, survival
Internet Requirement No Platforms macOS, Windows, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation, XBox

Subnautica:Below Zero is the sequel to the previous subnautica game. This time, you explore some very cold waters. Subnautica starts by dropping the player onto an empty planet with nothing but a crafting bench and a few supplies. The aim of the game is to find ways to survive in the very wet environment. The game has many biomes that you can go to with secrets in all of them. But be careful, because you don't know what might be swimming in the depths.

Genshin Impact
Price Free Rating 8/10
Company miHoYo Genres Action role-playing
Internet Requirement Yes Platforms Android, iOS, Windows, PlayStation

Welcome to the beutiful land of Teyvat! This world is split into seven nations with one god commanding each region. Collect your favorite characters through the wish system (But try to avoid getting a gambling addiction) and use those characters to face of against the many creatures of the world. Explore the many nations of Teyvat and discover whatever secrets hidden in this world.

Bendy and the Ink Machine
Price 1000 pesos Rating 9/10
Company Joey Drew Studios Genres Survival horror
Internet Requirement No Platforms Windows, macOS, Linux, PlayStation, XBox, Nintendo Switch, Android, iOS

Who knew your creations could lead to your destruction? The player controls Henry, an animator who returns to his old workplace. However, he ends up getting stuck in the building and the player must help him leave. But it seems that as you get closer to the end, the world gets stranger and stranger. What are thos things trying to kill you? What happened to your old workplace? And what in the world is "Bendy"?

Cytus 2
Price 100 Pesos for base game Rating 9/10
Company Rayark Genres Rhythm game
Internet Requirement No Platforms iOS, Android, PlayStation Mobile, Nintendo Switch

You wouldn't understand why rhytm gamers restart a whole level when they miss one note until you become one yourself. If ever you do try a rhythm game, Cytus 2 is a good place to start. In the game, you tap, hold, and drag notes along with the rhythm but unlike most rhythm games, instead of the notes moving to the line, the line moves to the notes. The game also includes many extras but it would be a good idea to wait for a sale before you get them.

The Witness
Price 2000 Pesos Rating 8/10
Company Thekla, Inc. Genres Puzzle
Internet Requirement No Platforms Windows, macOS, iOS, XBox, PS4

A circle, a path, and an end. You draw a a line from the circle to end. That's the game. So why is that so difficult? The witness is a puzzle solving game where you try to form a path that goes from one point to another. However, as you progress through the game, more and more rules get added that can turn a simple puzzle into a mind-boggling problem. All the puzzles in the game build up to the big question of this game. What are you doing on this island?
